# Product Overview

This project is a smart lifelog system that uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to automatically read scanned receipts. It functions as a household ledger, tracks past purchase history, and records daily activities for analysis. The goal is to provide users with insights into their consumption patterns and behavior to support smarter decision-making.

## Core Features

- **High-Precision OCR**: Extracts date, store name, items, and amounts from receipt images with high accuracy.
- **Automatic Ledger Generation**: Categorizes extracted information into household expense categories (e.g., food, transport).
- **Purchase History Analysis**: Tracks purchases by store and item, visualizing price fluctuations over time.
- **Behavioral Log Integration**: Connects purchase data with GPS location and calendar entries to map out daily activities.
- **Data Visualization**: Presents spending habits and behavioral patterns through graphs and charts.
- **Cloud Sync**: Synchronizes data across multiple devices with secure backup and restore capabilities.

## Target Audience

- Individuals who find manual bookkeeping tedious.
- Users wanting to understand and analyze their spending habits.
- People interested in logging their daily activities and lifestyle.
- Shoppers looking to make smarter purchasing decisions by tracking price changes.
